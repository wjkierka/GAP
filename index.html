GAP widget demo

This page includes a simple spreadsheet-like demo. To test the widget: 1) Click the GAP icon once to enter standby mode; 2) Drag the icon near a cell; 3) Click a cell (it will be highlighted) to set it as the target; 4) Click the GAP icon repeatedly to generate digits appended to the selected cell (demo: up to 6 digits).

Note: This demo works on same-origin pages. Integrations with external spreadsheets (Google Sheets etc.) require platform-specific APIs or browser extensions.
